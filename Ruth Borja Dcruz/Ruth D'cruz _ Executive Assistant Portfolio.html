Say Hello! 👋

Executive Assistant

I’m an Executive Assistant with two years of experience supporting visionary executives in the fintech and AI space. I partner with executives to help them:


  • Cut through the noise and make more informed decisions.

  • Proactively take tasks off their plate, encouraging 10x delegation to save time.

  • Shift from routine admin tasks to achieving their ambitious goals!


Values I cherish:

I take pride in my work, always investing my full effort into every task. I actively seek feedback and embrace opportunities for improvement. I'm not afraid to go above and beyond to support my executive. If there's something I can do to make things easier for you, I will do it or learn how to do it. I also enjoy tinkering with AI tools to simplify tasks, and it makes me super happy when my proactive ideas translate into successful outcomes. Celebrating my executive's milestones and supporting them through challenges is at the heart of what I do.


Outside of work, I love spending quality time with my family and experimenting with new cooking recipes. It’s my way of unwinding and getting inspiration. Feel free to share what recipe you love most! 😉
